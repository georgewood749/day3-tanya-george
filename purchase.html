The ingredients for a chili can be purchased fresh from most supermarkets.

You can also purchase most ingredients fresh from farmers' markets. Often much better quality and much fresher than supermarket products too.

If you have the patience and the skills, you can grow your own produce. Given enough practice, your produce will be far superior to any purchased in a supermarket or farmers' markets, and much fresher and cleaner too!
